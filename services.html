Our Professional Services

Tailored care for your furry friends

Hair Trimming

Gentle Grooming

Our expert groomers use pet-friendly clippers to carefully trim your dog’s coat based on breed and preference. We ensure your pet feels safe, comfortable, and looks fresh!

Bath & Blow Dry

Squeaky Clean

We use warm water and gentle, hypoallergenic shampoos to clean and soothe your dog’s skin. After the bath, we hand-dry with care, avoiding high heat to keep your pet stress-free.

Nail Clipping

Safe & Easy

Our team is trained in safely trimming nails with precision. We handle your dog’s paws gently to avoid discomfort and ensure a stress-free experience during every session.

Teeth Cleaning

Fresh Smile

We offer non-invasive teeth brushing using dog-safe toothpaste to reduce plaque and promote oral health. A fresh mouth equals a happy pup!

Ear Cleaning

Gentle Care

We gently clean your dog’s ears to prevent infections. Using soft wipes and veterinary-approved solutions, we keep your pet’s ears fresh and healthy.

Full Grooming Package

All-in-One Pamper

This complete service includes trimming, bath, nails, ears, and a final touch-up. Perfect for dogs needing a full refresh with love and care.
